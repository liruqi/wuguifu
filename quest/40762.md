# 黑铁亵渎者

**任务等级:** 60  
**最低等级:** 55  
**开始地点:** Bixxle Screwfuse (Tel'Abim Island east from Tanaris)  

## 任务目标
在泰拉比姆的比克瑟仓库，为比克瑟·螺熔收集一支黑铁步枪、一个熔岩冷凝器、一个复杂的奥金炮管和一个熔岩碎片。

## 任务提示
This quest requires to collect 4 items.\n1) Magma Condensor (Blackrock Depths in Magma Condensor Crate) \n2) Intricate Arcanite Barrel (Blackrock Spire in Intricate Arcanite Barrels container) its a little box on a big box in the middle of the room.\n3) Molten Fragment (Molten Core from Molten Destroyer).\n4) Dark Iron Rifle (crafted by Engineers).\nFiery Core(x3) found in Molten Core
