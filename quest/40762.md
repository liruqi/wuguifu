# 黑铁亵渎者

**任务等级:** 60  
**起始等级:** 55  
**开始地点:** Bixxle Screwfuse (Tel'Abim Island east from Tanaris)  

## 任务目标
在泰拉比姆的比克瑟仓库，为比克瑟·螺熔收集一支黑铁步枪、一个熔岩冷凝器、一个复杂的奥金炮管和一个熔岩碎片。

## 任务提示
This quest requires to collect 4 items. 1) Magma Condensor (Blackrock Depths in Magma Condensor Crate) 2) Intricate Arcanite Barrel (Blackrock Spire in Intricate Arcanite Barrels container) its a little box on a big box in the middle of the room. 3) Molten Fragment (Molten Core from Molten Destroyer). 4) Dark Iron Rifle (crafted by Engineers). Fiery Core(x3) found in Molten Core, and Enchanted Thorium Bars(x10).
