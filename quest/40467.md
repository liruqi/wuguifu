# 奥术傀儡核心

**任务等级:** 55  
**起始等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Radgan Deepblaze (after Redridge Mountains - Burning Steppes pass [76 / 68], west from alliance camp)  

## 任务目标
从黑石深渊的[傀儡统帅阿格曼奇](../npc/8983.md)处找到并收集奥术魔像核心，然后返回燃烧平原黑石小径的拉德根·深焰身边。

## 任务提示
This questline starts at Radgan Deepblaze next to Orvak Sternrock with quest 'Gaining Orvak's Trust'

## 任务奖励
- [Energized Golem Core](../item/60672.md)
