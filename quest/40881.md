# 邪恶让我这样做

**任务等级:** 46  
**起始等级:** 35  
**开始地点:** Orvan Darkeye (Gilneas - ruins of Greyshire [31.3 / 47.0])  

## 任务目标
在吉尔尼斯城找到“论血液的力量”，然后返回吉尔尼斯格雷郡遗址的奥万·黑眼身边。

## 任务提示
Questline starts at Deathstalker Alynna (Gilneas Stillward Church [57.3 / 39.6], inside) with quest 'Dead Until Dark'. 'On the Powers of Blood' book on the table behind Regent-Lady Celia Harlow and Regent-Lord Mortimer Harlow, next to the Harlow Family Chest[7]. You will get the reward finishing the next quest.
