# 征用一个核心

**任务等级:** 45  
**起始等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Kex Blowmaster (The Barrens; [45.7 / 83.6] goblin under the tent.  

## 任务目标
从奥达曼的古代宝藏中获得完好的动力核心。

## 任务提示
Intact Power Core [11], in the room with platinum disc behind the last boss in the chest behind the right pillar. Questline starts in Southern Barrens -&gt; Bael Modan -&gt; west side of the road to The Thousand Needles, accros Bael Modan Excavation. first quest can be taken at lvl 18. last at lvl 53.
