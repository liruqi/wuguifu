# 为教会奉献

**任务等级:** 60  
**最低等级:** 55  
**开始地点:** Heirophant Nerseus (Deadwind Pass  

## 任务目标
在卡拉赞外的小教堂为圣职者涅修斯收集15个奥术精华、20个幻影之尘和10个强效不灭精华。

## 任务提示
After finishing this quest you will be able to get a quest for head/leg enchants. For each of them you will need:\n1x Overcharged Ley Energy - random rare item loot from trash/boss in Karazhan\n6x Arcane Essence - random trash loot.
