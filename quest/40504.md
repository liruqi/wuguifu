# 新与旧之四

**任务等级:** 57  
**最低等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Karfang (Burning Steppes - Karfang Hold; ""[90.1 / 22.5]"" northeast corner of Burning Steppes).  

## 任务目标
冒险进入仇恨熔炉采石场，为卡方要塞的卡方清除暮光之锤存在。

## 任务提示
Kill the last boss Har'gesh Doomcaller ""[5]"".\nQuestline starts at Councilor Vargek (Burning Steppes - Karfang Hold; ""[90.0 / 22.7]"" northeast corner of Burning Steppes) with quest 'Of New and Old'.
