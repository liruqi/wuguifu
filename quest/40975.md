# 审判与幻影

**任务等级:** 46  
**起始等级:** 35  
**开始地点:** Angered Phantom (Gilneas -Glaymore Stead [52.9 / 27.9])  

## 任务目标
为了住在吉尔尼斯的格莱摩尔农场的被激怒的幽灵杀死吉尔尼斯城内的[萨瑟兰法官](../npc/61421.md)。

## 任务提示
You can find Angered Phantom inside building on the mountain. Entering Gilneas gates follow the mountain on your left(east), passing a field with windmills you will find a path to the sea, almost at the edge turn north follow the path(barely noticable).
