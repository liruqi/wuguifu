# 虎口拔牙

**任务等级:** 25  
**最低等级:** 15  
**开始地点:** Pierce Shackleton (Undercity - Magic Quarter [85.4 / 13.6])  

## 任务目标
在影牙城堡中找到迈雷纳斯的财产，并将其交给幽暗城中的皮尔斯·沙克尔顿。

## 任务提示
You find Melenas' Belongings at [10], a box on the floor between two left book shelves . Questline starts at Duke Nargelas (Tirisfal Glades - Glenshire, west of Tirisfal Glades). Quest reward you will get after finishing next quest.
