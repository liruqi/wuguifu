# 埃博米尔事务

**任务等级:** 45  
**最低等级:** 35  
**开始地点:** Joshua Ebonmere (Gilneas - Ebonmere Farm ""[49.5 / 31.1]""). Entering Gilneas gates follow the mountain on your left(east)
