# 埃博米尔事务

**任务等级:** 45  
**起始等级:** 35  
**开始地点:** Joshua Ebonmere (Gilneas - Ebonmere Farm [49.5 / 31.1]). Entering Gilneas gates follow the mountain on your left(east), in the field with windmills you will find Joshua Ebonmere.  

## 任务目标
为吉尔尼斯的埃博米尔农场的约书亚·埃博米尔杀死吉尔尼斯城内的达斯蒂万·布莱克考尔，并夺回埃博米尔的地契。

## 任务提示
Prequest 'Ebonmere Bat Infestation' and 'Ebonmere Worgen Infestation'. Dustivan Blackcowl [4] drops the Ebonmere Deed
