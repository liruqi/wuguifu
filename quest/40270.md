# 莫尔奥格危机之七

**任务等级:** 54  
**起始等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Insom'ni <The Great Hermit> (Kazon Island, north of Gillijim Isle [57.2 / 10.1])  

## 任务目标
冒险进入阿塔[哈卡](../npc/14834.md)神庙的深处，拿到阿塔莱神杖，把它带给英桑姆尼完成咒语。

## 任务提示
Atal'ai Rod from the little green wooden chest on the floor behind Jammal'an the Prophet [4]. Questline starts at Haz'gorg the Great Seer (Stranglethorn Vale - Gillijim's Isle(west from Booty Bay) - Maul'Ogg Refuge, inside of the southeast cave 78.[1 / 81].) You will get the reward finishing last quest in the questline.
