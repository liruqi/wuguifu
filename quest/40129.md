# 偷一个核心

**任务等级:** 45  
**起始等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Torble Sparksprocket (The Barrens; 48.[6 / 83] gnome with purple goggles under the tent, next to the dwarf)  

## 任务目标
从奥达曼的古代宝藏中获得完好的动力核心。

## 任务提示
Intact Power Core [11], in the room with platinum disc behind the last boss in the chest behind the right pillar. Questline starts in Southern Barrens -&gt; Bael Modan -&gt; a little north from the path to Bael'dun Keep under the tent. First quest can be attain at lvl 18, last at lvl 53
