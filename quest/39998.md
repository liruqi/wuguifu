# 部落防御者之斧

**任务等级:** 18  
**起始等级:** 15  
**开始地点:** Birgitte Cranston &lt;Portal Trainer&gt; (Thunder Bluff)  

## 任务目标
把一块偷来的墓碑带给十字路口的纳[加尔](../npc/12057.md)·死眼。

## 任务提示
You start this Questline at Nargal Deatheye (Crossroads [51.2 / 29.1]). This quest JUST TELEPORTS you to WESTFALL. You can either finish this quest and get the reward after completing questline or use it as westfall teleport retaking the quest.

## 任务奖励
- [Horde Defender's Axe](../item/40065.md)
