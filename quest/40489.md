# 攻击仇恨熔炉

**任务等级:** 57  
**起始等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Senator Granitebelt (Burning Steppes - Morgan's Vigil; [85.2 / 67.9]).  

## 任务目标
冒险进入仇恨熔炉采石场并在内部深处清除暮光之锤的存在，完成后返回铁炉堡的麦格尼·铜须国王那里。

## 任务提示
Kill the last boss Har'gesh Doomcaller [5]. Questline starts with quest 'Investigating Hateforge' at the same questgiver.

- [Sigil of Heritage](../item/60695.md)
- [Rubyheart Mallet](../item/60696.md)
