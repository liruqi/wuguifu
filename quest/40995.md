# 超越的力量

**任务等级:** 44  
**最低等级:** 38  
**开始地点:** Dark Bishop Mordren (Gilneas - Stillward Church [57.7 / 39.6])  

## 任务目标
前往剃刀高地，为吉尔尼斯寂静守卫教堂的黑暗主教莫德伦杀死那里的寒冰之王亚门纳尔，并带回他的魂器。

## 任务提示
Questline starts with quest 'Through Greater Magic' at Dark Bishop Mordren. Description in the questlog is wrong. at least it was for me. Amnennar the Coldbringer [6] drops Obsidian Phylactery. You will get the reward finishing the last quest in the chain.
