# 帮法杉的忙。

**任务等级:** 58  
**最低等级:** 55  
**开始地点:** Strahad Farsan (The Barrens - Ratchet; ""[62.6 / 35.5]"")  

## 任务目标
冒险进入通灵学院，为棘齿城的斯坦哈德·法杉找回《召炎控制术》一书。

## 任务提示
Questline starts at Craftsman Wilhelm (Eastern Plaguelands - Light's Hope Chapel) with quest 'A New Rune-Frontier'.\nYou will get this reward after finishing the last quest in the questline.
