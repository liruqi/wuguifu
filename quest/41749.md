# Stone Golem Salvage

**任务等级:** 28  
**最低等级:** 22  
**开始地点:** Kixxle (Wetlands; ""[50.2 / 37.7]"")  

## 任务目标
Acquire the runestone of a Crumbling Stone Golem inside Dragomaw Retreat and bring it to Kixxle on the mainroad in the Wetlands.

## 任务提示
The runestone drops from Crumbling Stone Golem inside the dungeon.
