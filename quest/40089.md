# 猖獗的原林熊怪

**任务等级:** 33  
**最低等级:** 32  
**开始地点:** Grol the Exile (Ashenvale, a bit north side the road between Astranaar and Felwood road T-Intersection, north from the furbolg camp; [56.1 / 59.2]).  

## 任务目标
冒险进入新月林地，从里面的熊怪那里为流放者戈卢尔收集8个原林徽记。

## 任务提示
Groveweald Badge dropes from any elite furbolg inside dungeon and in front of the entrance outside of dungeon.
