# 卡拉赞之谜之七

**任务等级:** 60  
**起始等级:** 58  
**开始地点:** Magus Ariden Dusktower (Deadwind Pass [52.4 / 34.3], northeast part of Deadwind Pass, north from eastern T-Intersection).  

## 任务目标
深入卡拉赞墓穴，为逆风小径的大法师艾瑞登·暗塔杀死墓穴看守阿拉鲁斯。

## 任务提示
Kill the last boss Alarus [7]. Questline starts at the same NPC Magus Ariden Dusktower in Deadwind Pass. You will get the key to open the entrance door finishing part 6 of the questline.

- [Orb of Kaladoon](../item/60464.md)
