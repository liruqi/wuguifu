# 护腕的上半部分之一

**任务等级:** 60  
**起始等级:** 55  
**开始地点:** Parnabus <Wandering Wizard> (Gilneas; [22.9 / 74.4], very south of Gilneas City, west of river. inside of a lonely hose).  

## 任务目标
为了吉尔尼斯的帕纳布斯从黑石塔的黑色龙人身上收集一块龙族能量。

## 任务提示
Highly recommend to take prequest 'The Binding of Xanthar' from Hanvar the Righteous (Deadwind Pass in the small church outside of Karazhan [40.9 / 79.3]). Reward for the last quest of The Upper Binding quest chain will be quest item 'The Upper Binding of Xanthar' for quest 'The Binding of Xanthar'.
