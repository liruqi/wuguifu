# 格雷迈恩之石

**任务等级:** 47  
**起始等级:** 38  
**开始地点:** Dark Bishop Mordren (Gilneas - Stillward Church [57.7 / 39.6])  

## 任务目标
为吉尔尼斯寂静守卫教堂的黑暗主教莫德伦找到午夜碎片

## 任务提示
Questline starts with quest 'Through Greater Magic' at Dark Bishop Mordren. Shard of Midnight is behind the last boss Genn Greymane [8] You will get the reward finishing the next quest.
