# 后备电源

**任务等级:** 34  
**起始等级:** 29  
**开始地点:** Technician Grimzlow (Durotar - Sparkwater Port [57.4 / 25.7]).  

## 任务目标
将诺莫瑞根的百万瓦电容器带给技术员格瑞姆兹鲁。

## 任务提示
Prequest 'A New Power Source' starts at Technician Spuzzle(Durotar - Sparkwater Port [57.4 / 25.8]) at level 7 . Megaflux Capacitor drops from Mekgineer Thermaplugg. You find Mekgineer Thermaplugg at [8]. He is the last boss in Gnomeregan. During the fight you have to disable the columns through pushing the button on the side.
