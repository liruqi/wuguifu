# 不明智的长老

**任务等级:** 34  
**起始等级:** 32  
**开始地点:** Grol the Exile (Ashenvale, a bit north side the road between Astranaar and Felwood road T-Intersection, north from the furbolg camp; [56.1 / 59.2]).  

## 任务目标
把“独眼”长老和喉长老的爪子从新月林地里拿出来，带给流放者戈卢尔。

## 任务提示
Paw of Elder 'One Eye' and Paw of Elder Blackmaw drops from minions of the first boss Grovetender Engryss [1] .
