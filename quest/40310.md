# 卡拉赞深渊之七

**任务等级:** 60  
**起始等级:** 58  
**开始地点:** Kor'gan (Swamp of Sorrows - Stonard; [44 / 4],54.6).  

## 任务目标
进入卡拉赞墓穴，为斯通纳德的科尔根杀死墓穴看守[阿拉鲁斯](../npc/91928.md)。

## 任务提示
Kill the last boss Alarus [7]. Questline starts at the same NPC Kor'gan in Stonard. You will get the key to open the entrance door finishing part 6 of the questline.

## 任务奖励
- [Charged Arcane Ring](../item/60459.md)
- [Tusk of Gardon](../item/60460.md)
- [Blackfire Orb](../item/60461.md)
