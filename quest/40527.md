# 乌克兹·沙顶的尽头

**任务等级:** 48  
**起始等级:** 40  
**开始地点:** Champion Taza'go (Tanaris - Sandmoon Village; northeast corner of Tanaris, northwest from Steamwheedle Port)

## 任务提示
The Questline starts with quest 'Sandfury Redemption I' at Seer Maz'ek in Sandmoon Village(Tanaris) [59.1 / 17.1].
