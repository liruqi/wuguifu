# 奥术武器

**任务等级:** 18  
**起始等级:** 14  
**开始地点:** Chok'Garok &lt;Stonemaul Clan&gt; (on a bank of Southfury River in the Barrens; [62.4 / 10.8])  

## 任务目标
从哀嚎洞穴中给卓克盖洛克带去5块月光木材、一块巨蛇水晶和一份变换精华。

## 任务提示
(Mage only) The Questline starts at Ureda &lt;Mage Trainer&gt; (Orgrimar) with quest 'Mastering the Arcane'. Moontouched Wood you get from trash mobs, a Crystal of the Serpent from Lord Serpentis &lt;Fanglord&gt;[10], and an Everchanging Essence from Lord Pythas &lt;Fanglord&gt; [7].

- [Spellweaving Dagger](../item/80861.md)
