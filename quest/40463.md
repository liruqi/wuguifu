# 真正的高级工头

**任务等级:** 51  
**起始等级:** 45  
**开始地点:** Orvak Sternrock (Burning Steppes - Blackrock Pass; [75.9 / 68.2]).  

## 任务目标
杀死巴古尔·黑锤，并为燃烧平原黑石小径的奥瓦克·严岩取回元老院命令。

## 任务提示
Questline starts at Radgan Deepblaze with quest 'Gaining Orvak's Trust' (Burning Steppes - Blackrock Pass; [76.1 / 67.6]). Kill Bargul Blackhammer and take Senate's Orders on the table next to the boss.
