# 监狱的线索

**任务等级:** 24  
**起始等级:** 18  
**开始地点:** Master Mathias Shaw <Leader of SI:7> (Stormwind - Old Town, Rogue district; [75.8 / 59.8])  

## 任务目标
深入暴风城监狱，并且找到有关马丁·柯林斯的信息。

## 任务提示
You can find Martin Corinth's Information inside Sealed Documents Crate [1] in the room across the dungeon entrance. Questline starts with quest 'Uncovering Mystery' at Lord Commander Ryke (Wetlands - Hawk's Vigil [36.4 / 67.3] under the tent) You will get reward after you finish the last quest of the questline.
